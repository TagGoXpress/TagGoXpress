FAQ

Q: Why use TagGoXpress instead of booking Speed Post myself?
A: We pick up from your door, handle booking & paperwork, provide a single contact for tracking and offer same-day service that Speed Post cannot match for urgent items.
Q: What items can't be sent?
A: Prohibited or hazardous items, perishables that require refrigeration, and high-value items without insurance. Contact us for clarifications.
Q: What if delivery fails?
A: We have defined re-delivery rules and will contact sender/receiver to reschedule. Refunds depend on the chosen service and reason for failure.
